✦ Chronicles of the Realm ✦

The Legend

Long ago, in a realm between worlds, a wandering fellowship of misfits discovered a fragment of ancient power — a force that had slept since before memory.

They built a sanctuary from wilderness and will. A place where every outcast could find purpose. Where heroes were forged in trial and legend was earned through deeds.

They called it Misfits — and it grew. Through dedication and mastery, warriors awakened their potential. Creatures born of aura and shadow rose to test them. And deep beneath the world, sealed ruins waited, full of ancient secrets and peril.

Today, the realm endures. Protected by its community. Shaped by its adventurers. And waiting — always waiting — for the next legend to begin.
